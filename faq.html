Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about MyOldCherokee.com and how the directory works.

What is MyOldCherokee.com?

MyOldCherokee.com is an independent buy and sell directory and resource hub for owners of older Cherokee models, with repair shop listings, junk/salvage yard listings, DIY/troubleshooting Guides, and community resources.

Does MyOldCherokee.com verify listings or sellers?

No. While listings and information are submitted in good faith and are believed to be accurate to the best of our knowledge, MyOldCherokee.com does not independently inspect or verify third-party listings, businesses, products, or services. Users are responsible for their own due diligence.

Can I report a suspicious or inaccurate listing?

Yes. Please do! We are building on a solid community that we are actually a part of so we want to give the same we hope for which is the very best. Users can flag listings for review. We will review and may remove listings at our discretion. Reporting, while it does not constitute verification or endorsement, it can help to improve the directory quality.

Does inclusion in the directory mean endorsement?

No. Inclusion in the directory does not mean endorsement, certification, or approval by MyOldCherokee.com.

How do I find a shop's phone number, address, or website from the directory?

We provide as much information as we are given by the person who submits it. Where available, we will also link directly to a business's own website as the first choice. If no website is listed, we link to a Google Maps location or Google search result so you can find directions, hours, and contact info. If neither is available, we link to a Yelp listing as a fallback. The goal is always to get you to a place where you can reach the business directly.

What are Guides?

Guides are owner-focused DIY and troubleshooting resources for older Cherokee models. They are provided in good faith and are accurate to the best of our knowledge, though they do not replace professional inspection or repair advice. Results may vary by vehicle, model year, and condition.

Are junk/salvage yard listings verified?

Salvage yard listings are submitted in good faith and are believed to be accurate to the best of our knowledge. MyOldCherokee.com does not independently verify inventory. Always confirm inventory, fitment, pricing, and policies directly with the yard.

What Cherokee models are covered?

MyOldCherokee.com focuses on older Cherokee models still on the road — or one good weekend away from it. The directory covers vehicles, parts, shops, and guides relevant to these models.

How do I list a vehicle, parts, shop, or salvage yard?

Use the Submit Listing page to submit your listing for inclusion in the directory. We review submissions and may reach out if we need additional information.

How do I get a listing updated or removed?

Use the Contact page and provide the listing name, the change needed, and your contact email. We will update or remove listings at our discretion.

Can I submit a guide or resource link?

Yes. Use the Contact page to suggest a resource, guide link, or original how-to content. All submitted content is reviewed and published in good faith.

Featured Businesses

Getting Featured on MyOldCherokee.com

Questions about featured placement, pricing, and what's included.

What does it mean to be a featured business?

Featured businesses are shops, suppliers, salvage yards, and fabricators that support the site and pay for elevated placement. Featured listings appear at the top of relevant pages and display an expanded card with your logo or initials, description, service area, phone number, map link, tags, and a verified badge. It's the best way to get found by Cherokee owners who are actively looking for a shop or parts source.

What are the pricing levels?

There are two levels of featured placement:

  • Level 1 — $39/month: Featured Businesses page, top of matching State page, top of matching Category page. Full business card with all fields.
  • Level 2 — $50/month: Everything in Level 1, plus Homepage carousel placement and Featured Vendor callout in blog posts.

Both plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime.

What's the difference between a free listing and a featured listing?

Free listings show your business name, city/state, and category with a basic link. Featured listings show a full card: logo or initials, description, service area with ZIP, tags, tap-to-call phone number, website link, Google Maps link, and a "Submitter verified" badge. Featured listings also appear at the top of relevant pages, above the free listings.

How does the homepage carousel work?

The homepage carousel (Level 2 only) puts your business card in a rotating section below the hero on the homepage. It's the first thing visitors see when they scroll past the search bar. Desktop users see 2–3 cards at a time with left/right controls. Mobile users can swipe horizontally.

What does "Submitter verified" mean?

The "Submitter verified" badge means the person who submitted the listing confirmed the location, phone number, and hours were accurate at the time of submission. It does not mean MyOldCherokee.com independently inspected or certified the business. We always recommend calling ahead before visiting.

How do I get started as a featured business?

Use the Get Featured page to choose your level and start the process. Or contact us directly with questions. We'll get your listing set up and live quickly.

Can I cancel or change my featured listing?

Yes. Featured placement is month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime or switch levels by contacting us. Changes typically take effect within one business day.
