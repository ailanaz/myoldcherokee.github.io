FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about MyOldCherokee.com and how the directory works.

What is MyOldCherokee.com?

MyOldCherokee.com is an independent buy and sell directory and resource hub for older Cherokee owners, with repair shop listings, junk/salvage yard listings, DIY/troubleshooting Guides, and community resources.

Does MyOldCherokee.com verify listings or sellers?

No. Listings and information are provided for reference only. MyOldCherokee.com does not inspect or verify third-party listings, businesses, products, or services. Users are responsible for their own due diligence.

Can I report a suspicious or inaccurate listing?

Yes. Users can flag listings for review. We may review and remove listings at our discretion. Reporting tools help improve directory quality but do not constitute verification or endorsement.

Does inclusion in the directory mean endorsement?

No. Inclusion in the directory does not mean endorsement, certification, or approval by MyOldCherokee.com.

What are Guides?

Guides are owner-focused DIY and troubleshooting resources for older Cherokee models. They are provided for general information only and do not replace professional inspection or repair advice.

Are junk/salvage yard listings verified?

No. Junk and salvage yard listings are provided for reference only. Always confirm inventory, fitment, pricing, and policies directly with the yard.

Is MyOldCherokee.com affiliated with Jeep or Stellantis?

No. MyOldCherokee.com is an independent site. Brand and model names are used for identification and compatibility reference only. All trademarks are the property of their respective owners.

What Cherokee models are covered?

MyOldCherokee.com focuses on older Cherokee models still on the road — or one good weekend away from it. The directory covers vehicles, parts, shops, and guides relevant to these models.

How do I list a vehicle, parts, shop, or salvage yard?

Use the Submit Listing page to submit your listing for inclusion in the directory. We review submissions and may reach out if we need additional information.

How do I get a listing updated or removed?

Use the Contact page and provide the listing name, the change needed, and your contact email. We will update or remove listings at our discretion.

Can I submit a guide or resource link?

Yes. Use the Contact page to suggest a resource, guide link, or original how-to content. All content is provided for reference only.

Still Have a Question?

Can't find your answer above? Reach out directly and we'll get back to you.
