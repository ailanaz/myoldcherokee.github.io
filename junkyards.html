Tips for Salvage Yard Visits

Before You Go

Know Your VIN

Confirm Cherokee vs. Cherokee Parts

Cherokee models share some parts but not many. Know your exact year and model before you go , certain engines, axles, and body clips are not interchangeable.

Bring Tools

Self-Service Yard Prep

Pull-a-part yards require your own tools. Bring basic sockets, wrenches, penetrating oil, and a flashlight. Call ahead to confirm Cherokee inventory before the trip.

Search Online First

Use Car-Part.com

Car-Part.com aggregates inventory from salvage yards nationwide. Search your part number before visiting to confirm availability and compare yard prices across your region.
