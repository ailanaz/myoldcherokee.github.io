Our Story

About MyOldCherokee.com

My Old Cherokee was created because owners of older Cherokees needed one place to buy, sell, find parts, locate repair shops, and access troubleshooting information, without digging through scattered forums and outdated listings.

MyOldCherokee.com is an independent buy and sell directory for older Cherokee models, with repair shop listings, junk/salvage yard listings, DIY/troubleshooting Guides, and community resources. Our goal is to organize listings and information in one clean, searchable place so older model Cherokee owners can find what they need.

We are starting with U.S. coverage, with plans to grow over time as the community grows. If you are an owner, a mechanic, or someone who refuses to give up on a great vehicle, you are in the right place.
