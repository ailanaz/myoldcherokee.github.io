Our Story

About MyOldCherokee.com

My Old Cherokee was created because the information needed to troubleshoot problems, handle DIY repairs, or even find a repair shop for older Jeep Cherokee XJs can be scattered all over the place — if you can find it at all.

This directory is meant to organize that information in one clean, searchable place so we can get our babies back on the road, off the road, or in the mud, where they belong. The goal is simple. Keep the most useful troubleshooting help, DIY resources, and Cherokee-savvy shops in one spot so the next owner can find real help in minutes, not hours.

We are starting with U.S. coverage, with plans to grow over time as the community grows. If you are an owner, a mechanic, or just someone who refuses to give up on a great vehicle, you are in the right place.
