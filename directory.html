Engine / Idle

The $8 IAC Fix Before You Buy a New One

Before replacing the idle air control valve, remove it and clean it with throttle body cleaner and a cotton swab. Fixes rough idle on a large number of 4.0s. Takes 20 minutes.

Overheating

Burp Your Cooling System the Right Way

Air pockets cause misleading temp gauge readings. Park nose-uphill, remove the rad cap (cold engine only), run it to temp with the heat on full blast, and top off slowly. Check for bubbles.

üìå Community Tip Beginner
Suspension

Check the Track Bar First for Death Wobble

Grab the track bar and try to wiggle it. Any movement at all means a worn mount or end , this is the single most common death wobble culprit on the XJ and the cheapest fix to start with.

üìå Community Tip Intermediate
Electrical

Clean Your Ground Straps Before Chasing Gremlins

Weird electrical issues on older Cherokees are often just bad grounds. Clean and re-torque all chassis-to-engine and body grounds before replacing sensors, modules, or pulling fuses.
