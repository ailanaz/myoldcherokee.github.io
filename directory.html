Before You Go to a Yard

Know Your VIN

Confirm the Right Cherokee Parts

Know your exact year and model before you go - certain engines, axles, and body clips are not interchangeable across Cherokee generations.

Bring Tools

Self-Service Yard Prep

Pull-a-part yards require your own tools. Bring sockets, wrenches, penetrating oil, and a flashlight. Call ahead to confirm Cherokee inventory.

Search First

Use Car-Part.com Before You Drive

Car-Part.com aggregates inventory from salvage yards nationwide. Search your part number first to confirm availability before making the trip.
