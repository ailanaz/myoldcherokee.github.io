Fuse diagrams by year: 1984–1996 and 1997–2001 quick links

A quick page that sends you to the correct fuse diagram for your Cherokee XJ year range.

When to use this guide: You need the fuse layout fast and want the right diagram without searching.

Overview

Use this page to go straight to the correct fuse diagram for your Cherokee XJ year range. No searching, just pick your years and click.

Find your year range

  • 1984–1996 XJ: One interior fuse box. Use the 1984–1996 diagram link below.
  • 1997–2001 XJ: Interior fuse box plus an under-hood Power Distribution Center (PDC). Use both diagram links below.

Quick steps

  1. Find your year range above.
  2. Open the matching diagram link.
  3. Locate the fuse or relay for the system that stopped working.
  4. If you need help testing the fuse, see the How to test a fuse correctly guide.
  5. If the fuse keeps blowing after you replace it, see the Fuse keeps blowing guide.
